Why Choose Us?

Domus Mea Property Finance Ltd have over 60 years’ combined experience in independent mortgage services throughout London and Surrey.

The financial market, with its myriad of lenders and products, can be daunting and stall decision-making. We like to think we do things a bit differently at Domus Mea. We’ll use our industry expertise to match you with a tailored product from a selection of the best – while keeping things as simple as possible for you.

No situation is ever the same and at Domus Mea we believe that everybody should be treated as an individual and given bespoke mortgage advice to suit their particular circumstances.

Whether you are employed, sole trader, partnership, LLP, Limited Company director or even a Freelancer, we have lenders that can accommodate your situation. Domus Mea have a wealth of experience in placing mortgages for the ‘risk takers’ that have taken the leap of faith and set up as self-employed.

Customer service is at the heart of our company here at Domus Mea. We really take care of our customers and ensure that they receive a great mortgage advice. Our hardworking moneyman team works day in, day out to make sure that you feel happy with everything that is going on during every stage of the mortgage process.

We believe in jargon free, always friendly people and an appointment that is convenient for you either over the phone or in our private meeting room at our office or in the comfort of your own home. You will have a named advisor who will monitor the progress of your mortgage and provide you with regular updates and will communicate with them directly - no call centers!
